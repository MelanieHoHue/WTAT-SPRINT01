Project Idea: Yet another TODO APP

But one with more features, that makes the app to a more sophisticated one.

At the start page you can see all the topic you´re working on along with their sub-topics.

As well as all of the TODO´s you have to do for any of your working topics.

In addition with your bookmark collection as a whole, that represent the results of all of the research you did.
If you click on a topic you get a handy overview of the belonging

  • Sub-Topics>
  • ToDo´s
  • (eventually) their Due Dates
  • and linked Bookmarks
Within the ToDo-view you can take notes, save attachments on the behalf of the ToDo.

You can even see a little statistic about e.g.:

  • when you have worked on it
  • and how long did it takes you
It would be possible to develop even more overviews and statistics. E.g.:

  • how does the topic integrate into the superior project
  • how is the project progress performing
  • when will milestones be reached
